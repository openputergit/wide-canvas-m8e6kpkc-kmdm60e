Frequently Asked Questions

What is the minimum installment amount?

The minimum installment amount is ₹2,000, and payments can be made in multiples of ₹2,000.

Can I redeem the scheme for cash?

No, this scheme is exclusively for jewelry purchases.

What happens if I miss a payment?

Missed payments may affect your scheme benefits. Please contact our customer support for assistance.

How do I claim the special discount?

The special discount (up to 100% of the first installment value) will be applied automatically during redemption in the 13th month.
